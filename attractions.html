The Khan el-Khalili Bazaar in Cairo: A Travel Guide to ... The Khan el-Khalili Bazaar in Cairo is a bustling, vibrant market that has been around for centuries and is a must-see for anyone visiting the city. Located in the heart of Islamic Cairo, this colorful bazaar is filled with shops, stalls, restaurants, and cafes selling everything from spices to jewelry to souvenirs.
